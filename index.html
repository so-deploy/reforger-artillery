Spearhead Artillery Calculator i
About the calculator:

Ballistic tables provided for grad are limited to arbitrary range (in reality it can shoot up to 10.6km), and are inaccurate (always undershoot). In this calculator i fix these problems.

Tables for Howitzer are pretty accurate, i just plugged them as they are.

Coordinate Input Guide:

XY Grid (3-digits): 050 080
Grid position, snaps to center by default, finetuned by numpad values, can be read from under the cursor on the map

XY Direct (4+ digits): 0500 0825
Precise position, can be acquired by using laser on the drone, or eyeballing on the map (grid 50 would be 5000, values increase up and right)

Z Value: 050 080 120
Elevation, always direct, read from under the cursor on the map

Numpads: 050 080 120 94 (Numpad 94)
Use numpad grid subdivision to fine-tune the position within the grid (can be used as many times as you like)
